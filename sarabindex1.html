About Me

I am a computer science postgraduate with a Master’s in Computer Applications (MCA), graduating in 2012. My career journey is shaped by a passion for both technology and creativity. I hold an ISTQB certification, earned in 2014, which reflects my commitment to quality and precision in software testing. Beyond my technical expertise, I am an author with three books to my name, driven by a passion for sharing knowledge and insights. I am also deeply engaged in music, particularly in the spiritual practice of kirtan. I enjoy playing the harmonium, both by ear and with sheet music, and one of my favorite kirtans is "Jeevat Jeevat Jeevat Raho," which I love performing with newly learned tunes. Following traditions is important to me, and I regularly participate in kirtan sessions at gurudwara sahib. I am a lifelong learner, enthusiastic about programming—especially in Java and Python development—and always eager to embrace new technologies. Whether through coding, writing, or music, I am driven by a desire to grow, create, and contribute.
